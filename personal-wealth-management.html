Index Strategy Retirement Guide

This guide is designed to help you understand how an Index Strategy (commonly known as a Fixed Indexed Annuity or FIA) can provide market-linked growth potential with principal protection, making it a popular option for today’s uncertain market.
What is an Index Strategy?

An Index Strategy is a retirement tool that links your potential growth to a stock market index (like the S&P 500), without directly investing in the market. It combines the opportunity for growth with downside protection.

Key Benefits
  • Protection from Market Losses
  • Tax-Deferred Growth
  • Lifetime Income Options
  • Potential for Legacy Planning
  • No Direct Market Exposure
How It Works

You select a strategy tied to an index. If the index goes up, you earn interest based on that growth (up to a cap or participation rate). If the index goes down, your account doesn’t lose value due to market performance.

Is It Right For You?
  • You’re concerned about market risk
  • You want growth potential without direct market loss
  • You’re planning for long-term retirement income
  • You want to protect and preserve your principal
