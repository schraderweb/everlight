Key Terms Simplified
  • Rider: An optional add-on to your annuity that provides additional benefits like income or death protection.
  • Fees: Charges taken out of your account to cover features or management costs.
  • Surrender Fee: A penalty you may pay if you take out money too early from your annuity.
  • Death Benefit: The amount paid to your beneficiaries if you pass away while the annuity is active.
  • Liquidity: Refers to how much access you have to your money and how easily you can withdraw it.
